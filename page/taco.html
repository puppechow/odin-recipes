Meat Taco

Description

The meat taco wonderfully balances all food groups to give its eaters the perfect amount of grain, meat, and other. This recipe is the simplest to make and, like the burger, is a great choice for casual backyard festivities. With no useless ingredients, this recipe is a Generic Brand™ favorite.

Ingredients

  • Generic Brand™ Taco Bun
  • Generic Brand™ Meat Log
  • Generic Brand™ Yellow Sauce

Preparation

  1. Open the Generic Brand™ Taco Bun so that there is ample room to place ingredients inside.
  2. Cook the Generic Brand™ Meat Log using a heating instrument such as a grill, microwave, or Generic Brand™ Meat Log Cooker.
  3. Place the meat log in the Generic Brand™ Taco Bun.
  4. Top the meat taco with Generic Brand™ Yellow Sauce for added flavor.

Your taco is now ready for consumption.
